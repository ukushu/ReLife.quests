* Visit the dentist for a routine examination
* If necessary, start treating your teeth
* After each visit to the dentist for positive reinforcement, please yourself with something tasty - go to a restaurant, McDonald's, KFC, sushi, etc. 

!!! Even if you have little money, REWARDS ARE IMPORTANT. This will help you have less moral resistance for your next trip to the dentist.

To avoid serious problems, you should visit your dentist for a checkup every six months. 
You may be forced to have calculus cleanings, but frequent cleanings can cause calculus to build up even faster. Try not to have cleanings more than once a year. Less often is better.
