Pap test = cytological screening.

It is necessary to protect yourself from cervical cancer in the early stages, not when it is too late.

The Pap test should be done every 3 years, starting at the age of 21.

If you have risk factors for cervical cancer, it is recommended to do this test once a year.

RISK FACTORS:
* smoking
* HIV INFECTION
* weakened immunity due to corticosteroids
* frequent change of sexual partners without using condoms
* cervical cancer in the mother/grandmother/sister
* beginning of sexual relations before the age of 16
