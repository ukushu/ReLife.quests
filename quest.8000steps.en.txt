The benefits of 8000 steps a day:
* Improved heart health
* Weight control. Walking is no less effective in losing weight than cardio exercises and weightlifting
* Improved mood - walking increases the production of endorphins
* Reducing the risk of type 2 diabetes - maintaining the correct blood glucose level
* Strengthening the muscles of the legs and pelvis
* Reduced stress and anxiety (mental health)
* Improving sleep
