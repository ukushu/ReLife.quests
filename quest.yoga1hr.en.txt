If you don't have the money for a studio class with a trainer, you can pay attention to the Yoga | Down Dog program. 

This program:
* Has support for 11 languages 
* And very decent in quality 
* Suitable for both beginners and advanced practitioners
* Many types of yoga with different intensity of body load
* Flexible control over the duration of the class: 9-80 min.
* It is possible to work offline (will work without the Internet)
* There is a statistics management
* Very simple interface
* Ability to choose the type of background music and the volume ratio between the background music and the voice telling you what to do
